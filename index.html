Dmitry Zhimirikin

Facade Engineer with over 10 years of experience in the design and delivery of faсade systems for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Specialized in aluminium faсade systems, design stages K2 and K3, detailed technical documentation, and on-site technical support. Proven experience in project execution and project management.

Profile

Design engineer with more than 10 years of experience in façade systems and building envelope design. Education: Leningrad Mechanical Institute (Baltic State Technical University), Master’s degree; diploma and professional qualification officially recognized by the Estonian ENIC/NARIC Centre (2025).

Professional practice in the Russian Federation (Saint Petersburg); since 2021 — working in Estonia, managing projects in both Estonia and Russia.

Six years of experience within system supplier companies: Reynaers Aluminium (Saint Petersburg), Alutech (Saint Petersburg), Inicial (Saint Petersburg).

Project management experience, including participation in a major international project — Lakhta Center, faсade design of the Multifunctional Building (MFB) as part of Samsung C&T Corp.

In design practice, worked with Reynaers, Aluprof, and Alutech systems (primary), as well as Schüco, Tatprof, KRAMZ, Inicial, frameless glazing systems by Kinlong, and others. Delivered projects with ventilated façades based on aluminium cassette systems and three-dimensional ceramic cladding (NBK).

2D drafting (including dynamic blocks and LISP), 3D modelling, structural calculations, and full-cycle design documentation: steel structures (KM), detailed drawings, fabrication and installation schedules, sheet metal cutting layouts, including complex-shaped cassette elements.

English — B1 (working proficiency, mainly spoken), Estonian — A2.

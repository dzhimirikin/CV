Dmitry Zhimirikin

Facade Engineer with over 10 years of experience in the design and delivery of faсade systems for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings. Specialized in aluminium faсade systems, design stages K2 and K3, detailed technical documentation, and on-site technical support. Proven experience in project execution and project management.

Profile

Design engineer with more than 10 years of experience in faсade systems and building envelope design. Education: Leningrad Mechanical Institute (Baltic State Technical University), Master’s degree; diploma and professional qualification officially recognized by the Estonian ENIC/NARIC Centre (2025).

Professional practice in the Russian Federation (Saint Petersburg); since 2021 — working in Estonia, managing projects in both Estonia and Russia.

Six years of experience within system supplier companies: Reynaers Aluminium (S.-Petersburg), Alutech (S.-Petersburg), Inicial (S.-Petersburg).

Project management experience, including participation in a major international project — Lakhta Center, faсade design of the Multifunctional Building (MFB) as part of Samsung C&T Corp.

In design practice, worked with REYNAERS, ALUPROF, ALUTECH systems (primary), as well as SCHÜCO, TATPROF, KRAMZ, INICIAL, frameless glazing systems by Kinlong, and others. Delivered projects with ventilated faсades based on aluminium cassette systems and three-dimensional ceramic cladding (NBK).

Participated in testing of facade structures: annealing of structures at SERCONS SZRC, Russia, and operational tests at IFT Rosenheim, Germany.

2D drafting (including dynamic blocks and LISP), 3D modelling, structural calculations, and full-cycle design documentation: working drawings of faсade structures and connection details (K2), detailed drawings (K3), fabrication and installation specifications, sheet metal layouts, including complex-shaped cassette elements.

English — B1 (working proficiency, mainly spoken), Estonian — A2.

Own fully equipped workstation.
